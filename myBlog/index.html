Welcome back to this blog on AWS services:
Cloud services mainly comprise of
1) Compute capacity
2) Storage and
3) Networking
Big enterprises and business used to install their own small, medium to huge data centers as per the requirement. The entities who have to concenrate on their busines strategy and problems are compelled to look after the phisical eshtablishment of data center man power maintenance restoration of breakdown to ensure availability of services. Cloud services takes care of their data center needs and make them highly available scalable and flexible on demand. AWS has 25 region each region have two or more availability zones which are phisically apart with independent resources supported by 218+ edge location world wide to make data, information readily available to the customers reducing latency period. Amazon web services are available to all sort of customers managing large peta byte data to tiny personal needs seving nearly 245 countries.
